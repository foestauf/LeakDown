=== CONFIGURATION ===

Two independent sliders in Unity Mod Manager:
• Steam Leakdown Rate: 0-500% (default 100% = ~10%/hour)
• Brake Leakdown Rate: 0-500% (default 100% = ~10%/hour)

NOTE: Actual leak rate automatically adjusts based on locomotive condition!
A damaged loco at 100% setting will leak faster than a restored loco at 100%.

=== COMPATIBILITY ===
